The Pact System

How Wick
actually works.

Wick isn't a blocker. It isn't a timer. It's a two-person commitment where the streak breaks the second either of you does. Here's the exact mechanic — no fluff.

01
Write one sentence about yourself
Not a goal. Not a rule. An identity statement — "I am someone who is present with the people I love." Wick stores it and shows it back every time you reach for your phone. Not as judgment. As a mirror made from your own words.
IDENTITY LAYER
02
Set an opens limit — not screen time hours
You pick how many times you can open a specific app per day. Why opens and not time? Because doom scrolling is 40 x 3-minute sessions, not one long one. Opens is the honest metric. Wick tracks this automatically — no manual logging.
OPENS TRACKING
03
Invite one person you don't want to disappoint
They accept, set their own opens limit, and you share one streak counter. Both of you see each other's real-time opens on the home screen. The visibility alone changes behaviour — before you even hit your limit.
ACCOUNTABILITY LAYER
04
The streak breaks the moment — not at midnight
This is the only thing that separates Wick from everything else. Snapchat resets at midnight. Wick breaks the second you cross your limit. Your partner gets a push notification immediately. Not a weekly report. Right then. That immediacy creates actual stakes.
INSTANT BREAK — THE KEY MECHANIC
05
You can still open the app — but they'll know
Wick never blocks you. When you hit your limit, the intercept screen shows your identity sentence and two buttons: Close Instead (large, obvious) and Open Anyway (small, dim). Tap Open Anyway and your partner is notified instantly. No punishment. Just visibility. That's enough.
OVERRIDE SYSTEM
06
2 grace days per month — life happens
Bad days exist. Wick gives each person 2 grace days per month where crossing the limit doesn't break the streak. But your partner can see when a grace day was used. That visibility keeps the system honest without being punishing.
